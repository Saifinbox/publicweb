Enabling developers to create apps with your data

ODP API toolbox enables developers to create apps that
showcase your data in exciting new ways.

Here are some of the use cases illustrating the impact of Open Data worldwide.

AflaMD

Website with the planned expenditures for all the schools in Moldova
http://afla.md
Country: Moldova
Sector: Education
Impact: Contributes to the increase of transparency in public education spending

Data Used: Public spending on schools and educational resources from the
Ministry of Education of Moldova

Aidin

A website that improves the patient-placement process in
choosing a post-acute provider

http://www.myaidin.com/
Country: United States
Sector: Healthcare
Impact: Reduces asymmetric information between patients and
post-acute care providers

Data Used: Health data from the U.S. Department of Health
and Human Services: Nursing Home Compare, Home Health
Compare, Patient Outreach and Hospital Integration

Budeshi

Website that provides access to public budget and
procurement data in Uganda

http://www.budeshi.ug
Country: Uganda
Sector: Governance
Impact: Improves access to public procurement and budget
information

Data Used: Procurement and budget data from Uganda
Ministry of Energy, Oil and Mineral Development and Uganda's
Ministry of Transport

Centre for Governance and Public Accountability (CGPA)


Civil society organization working for promotion of public
accountability and good governance in Pakistan

http://www.c-gpa.org/
Country: Pakistan
Sector: Governance

Impact: Informs citizens on key governance issues and strengthens
state response to citizens’ grievances

Data Used: District level education budget of various districts in
Pakistan; Municipal budget data of Peshawar District; Education,
health, police, legal data from Khyber Pakhtunkhwa District

Chequeado

A fact-checking service that monitors political activities in Argentina

http://chequeado.com/
Country: Argentina
Sector: Media and communications

Impact: Enforces accountability in political discourse

Data Used: Public safety data from the Ministry of Public Safety and
Attorney General of the Province of Buenos Aires; Legal data from
Argentina’s Ministry of Justice; Demographics and social data from
Argentina’s Census; Economics statistics from Argentina’s National
Directorate of External Sector Statistics

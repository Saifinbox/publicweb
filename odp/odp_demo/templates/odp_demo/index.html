Creating social and economic value through Open Data
We connect, equip and inspire people, industries, government and not for profit organization to innovate with open data

STORE AND MANAGE DATA

Store the raw data and metadata. Visualize structured data with interactive tables, graphs and maps. Get statistics and usage metrics for your datasets. Search geospatial data on a map by area.

PUBLISH AND FIND DATASETS

Publish datasets via import or through a web interface. Search by keyword or filter by tags. See dataset information at a glance. Full change history lets you easily undo changes or view old versions.

ENGAGING WITH THE USERS AND ECOSYSTEM AT LARGE

Use Open Data Portal Pakistan to collect data from multiple sources, break data silos, share interactive data visualizations, foster social applicative development through APIs and collaborate with their key public sector organizations.

CUSTOMIZE AND EXTEND

Use the API‘s rich programming interface, and benefit from over 60 extensions including link checking, comments, and analytics. Easy to use Restful APIs allow secure and fast data communication between applications, with rich quering.

Collaborate with the national open government community to create greater transparency and accountability, increase citizen engagement, and drive innovation and economic opportunities through open data, open information, and open dialogue.

Open Data’s true power can only be harnessed when it is accessible to all, regardless of technical expertise. In making data easy to publish and understand, communities can become collaborative ecosystems and are empowered to co-create ways to better their quality of life.


ODP version 1.0 let’s users upload a data set in less than a minute, browse data by categories, regions and share it with the rest of the world. Next prototype features include sourcing API data and request data of various public and private organisations.
